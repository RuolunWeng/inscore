16. Scripting

Since INScore saves its states as text files containing lists of OSC messages, it becomes natural to design INSCore scenes using these messages as a textual scripting language. For a better support of scripting, a loop operation is introduced to generate messages in sequence and variables are introduced to provide a better parameters control.
